Admissions

Welcome to the admissions page. Here you can find information about the application process, eligibility criteria, and important dates.

How to Apply

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Upload required academic documents.
  3. Submit the application before the deadline.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Intermediate / A-Level or equivalent qualification.
  • Minimum academic requirements as defined by the department.

Important Dates

Admissions open in May and close by the end of June each year.
